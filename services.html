Window Treatment

Window Treatment

New

Our services include custom made draperies, advice in style, colour and design for your window treatments, measuring, hardware and installation. We also distribute a wide selection of blinds which include horizontal and vertical blinds, roller shades,  blackouts, sun-screens, sheer horizontal shades, high-lite shades, roman shades, shutters,  honeycomb cellular shades, pleated shades, among others. Most of these options are available in the latest in motorized systems and home automation.
